Hello World!

Hi, I am Nick Kelly! I currently work at Intel in Hillsboro as a CPU architect doing performance modeling. In general, I am interested in low-energy and resilient architectures, micro-architecture, graphics, and compilation techniques. Unprofessionally, I enjoy rock climbing, running, drawing, and anything outdoor related.

Recently, I graduated from University of Texas at Austin with a M.S. in Computer Architecture and Embedded Systems (May 2016). While there, I worked on error modeling and injection in LPH, led by Mattan Erez. I did my undergraduate studies at Oregon State University in Electrical and Computer Engineering (June 2013). During my final year, I focused on topics relating to computer engineering and analog/circuit design.

Additionally, I have done a lot work in web development—spanning multiple languages, frameworks, and devices. Please see my resume, projects, and publications for more information.
